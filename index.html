How You Met

Haha!!!! What an interesting question. Alright, our first meeting was online through social media in 2020 during lockdown.
It all started with me viewing her Facebook page, and she looked familiar until it flashed back that we shared the same primary school.
I tried texting her before at first, though she didn't reply. I really appreciated that she was a beautiful girl and hoped for a chance to talk to her.
It took time for me to get that chance. One day, I woke up and I was added to some WhatsApp group of OBz and OGz 2017. At the moment, I was lost and couldn't flow with their jazz, but as I continued to scroll through the previous chat texts, I saw her photo introducing herself as Shanitah.
I was excited, and on checking the account information, it was her!!! I slid into her DMs and said, "hello" with a waving hand emoji. She replied to me, and I introduced myself, and from there, our story continues up to date.
